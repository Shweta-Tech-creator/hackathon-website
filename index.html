What is

Buildhathon 4.0?

Buildhathon 4.0 is an innovative national-level hackathon hosted by ITM Skills University. This 3-day event aims to unite talented students from across the country, empowering them to apply their creativity and problem-solving skills to real-world challenges in software and hardware development. The hackathon fosters collaboration, learning, and technical growth while providing a platform for groundbreaking solutions that address modern societal and industry needs.

Ideation

Participants come together to form teams and brainstorm innovative, impactful solutions to real-world challenges. This phase emphasizes creativity, problem identification, and conceptual clarity to lay a strong foundation for the project.

Development

Over an intense 48-hour period, teams transform their ideas into tangible prototypes using software coding, hardware assembly, or a combination of both. This phase involves continuous testing, debugging, and refining to build a functional and polished product.

Mentoring

Experienced industry professionals and academic mentors provide expert guidance throughout the hackathon. They help teams overcome technical hurdles, improve design and functionality, and craft compelling pitches to effectively communicate their solutions.

Final Pitch

In the concluding stage, teams present their projects to a distinguished panel of judges. They demonstrate the innovation, feasibility, and potential societal impact of their solutions, competing to earn recognition and exciting prizes.

What is the maximum number of members allowed in a group? ▼

A team can have a maximum of 4 members.

What are the arrangements for accommodation? ▼

Accommodation will be provided to all outstation participants free of cost. Details regarding the location and facilities will be shared via email after registration.

What food arrangements are in place for the hackathon? ▼

All registered participants will be provided with meals and refreshments throughout the event duration, including breakfast, lunch, dinner, and snacks. Vegetarian options will be available. Please inform us in advance about any specific dietary requirements.

Will there be any mentorship sessions during the hackathon? ▼

Yes, experienced mentors from industry and academia will be available throughout the event to guide teams, provide feedback, and help with technical or strategic challenges. Mentorship sessions will be both scheduled and on-demand.
